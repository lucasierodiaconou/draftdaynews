2021-2022 LBL Draft Recap!


The LBL Draft has concluded and it had many memorable moments. It was our first year where we had everyone in the same room for the draft, and it went very smoothly thanks to great planning by Commisioner Matt Orchanian. Thanks to the film crew who helped document the amazing evening, Alex Martin, Kate Ingles, and Julia Shpiz.

The draft itself was very interesting, as the coaches' months of scouting came to fruition with some great draft picks. The first round went as expected, with reigning MVP Aidan Roy going first overall to the Orienta Orioles. Rookies like Nate Bard, Joe Sarkozi, and Nate McMillan meant coaches needed to do additional scouting to determine the talent level of each player, and each coach hopes they got some great value picks. Only time will tell!
